scary apiary was formed in 2023 by Riley Jamison and Caleb Neal. Hailing out of Indiana, they play a blend of punk, emo, with a sometimes classic rock spin. Riley and Caleb have been playing music together for many years, but it wasn't until after high school when they decided to come together as a band. They have recently recorded an album in their garage, and it is entirely self-produced. So watch out, because that album is releasing on March 1st!
